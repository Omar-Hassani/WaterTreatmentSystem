# 💧 WaterTreatmentSystem

An advanced, industrial-grade web management platform designed specifically for **RO (Reverse Osmosis)** and **UF (Ultrafiltration)** water treatment plants. This platform effectively bridges the gap between chemical engineering processes and modern software automation.

The system empowers chemical engineers, plant managers, and maintenance technicians to monitor critical water parameters, log laboratory analytics, and track maintenance operations from a unified dashboard, while fully complying with **ISO 9001 Quality Management Standards**.

---

## 🚀 Key Capabilities

### 🧪 1. Water Quality & Laboratory Analytics
* **Parameter Logging:** Track physical and chemical parameters such as TDS, pH, conductivity, and flow rates.
* **Trend Monitoring:** Keep historical records of raw water versus product water quality to detect membrane degradation or fouling early.

### 🛠️ 2. Smart Maintenance Logs (Dual-Currency Supported)
* **Technical Maintenance Tracking:** Document all preventive, routine, and emergency maintenance actions taken at the station.
* **Dual-Currency Budgeting:** Built-in support for multiple currencies (**USD $** & **SYR ل.س**), allowing plant operators to choose their billing and cost tracking dynamically for parts or local labor.

### 📉 3. ISO 9001 Quality Control
* Designed with traceability and structure to ensure operational data logs meet rigorous international quality management requirements.

## 💻 Tech Stack & Architecture

* **Framework:** .NET Core / ASP.NET Core MVC (Modern & Scalable Architecture)
* **Design Patterns:** Repository Pattern and Unit of Work (for stable data persistence and cart logic)
* **Database:** SQL Server / Entity Framework Core (Code-First Migration)
* **Front-End UI:** Bootstrap 5, Razor Pages, Custom Responsive Dashboards, and Bootstrap Icons
